# E-Commerce Product Analysis

## Business Problem
Analyze 100,000+ real orders from a Brazilian e-commerce 
platform to identify top performing product categories, 
revenue trends, and pricing insights for better business decisions.

## Key Findings
- Total revenue: **BRL 19.54M** across **96K orders**
- **Bed & Bath and Beauty & Health** drive the highest order volumes
- **PCs and Fixed Telephony** have the highest avg order value (~BRL 1,000+)
- Revenue grew consistently from **Jan 2017 to mid-2018**
- Average order value across all categories: **BRL 216**
